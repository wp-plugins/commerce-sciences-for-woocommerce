Conversion optimization and personalization service for your online business or WooCommerce store.

== Description ==

Create, manage and analyze campaigns by targeting specific audiences and traffic sources with a simple yet powerful rule editor.

Customize your own message from scratch or edit one of our beautiful templates designed by Behavioral scientists and marketing experts.

Integrates seamlessly with your WooCommerce store, but can also run equally well on any Wordpress site.

== Installation ==

Install the plugin from the plugins directory or upload the commercesciences-wp-woo from inside this zip file to to your wp-content\plugins folder on your server.

You should have:
__wordpress_root_folder/wp-content/plugins/commercesciences-wp-woo/commercesciences-wp-woo.php
__wordpress_root_folder/wp-content/plugins/commercesciences-wp-woo/images/about-cs.jpg

Activate the plugin and click on settings to complete your registration.

Note that your site will not be affected until you complete registration and launch a campaign.
